Indoor
- Those games which are played in the closed environment, they are called as indoor games. Indoor games are played in homes, hotels, Malls, party halls etc. It includes games such as table tennis, video games, Wrestling etc.
Outdoor
- Those games which are played in an open environment, such games are termed as outdoor games. Out door games are played in the stadium which are game specific. Football is played in the foot ball court, basketball in basket ball court etc.
